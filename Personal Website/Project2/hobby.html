Watching Movies & TV-Series

I love watching Movies & TV-Series because they're a great form of entertainment and a great form of learning new things. Movies & TV-Series allow me to feel different emotions such as happines, saddnes, thrill, shock and all those mixed emotions are a reason I love watching them.

Another reason I love watching Movies & TV-Series is that they make me connect with fictional characters I’ve never met before, allow me to empathize with them while I watch them struggle or even when I watch them having fun.

Movies are also a good way to change my current emotional state, if I'm feeling down, I just put on a happy or cheerful movie to lighten my mood, or if I need to laugh, I just throw on an amusing comedy movie. Films always manage to cheer me up and that’s another reason why I love them.

One of the best things about movies & tv-series is that they allow me to escape reality for a certain time and it allows me to take a momentary break from life, and immerse myself into a different world, whether it's a world where a lot of action is going on, or a world full of crime.
